## What is a Trip Report?

A **trip report** documents a particularly effective, creative, or unexpected use of plurigrid/asi skills. Think of it as a field report from the frontier of compositional AI.

### What Makes a Good Trip Report?

1. **Specificity** - Which skills? Which voices? Which seeds?
2. **Reproducibility** - Can others follow your path?
3. **Surprise** - Did the composition reveal something unexpected?
4. **Conservation** - Is GF(3) maintained across the journey?
